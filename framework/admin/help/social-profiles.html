You can manage your social profiles and networks in the Social Profiles section. The most popular ones are already created for you, just change the URL to your own and save!

The saved social profiles will show up in the "Social Icons" tab of the MIXT Elements metabox on the Menus page. They will also be displayed in the secondary navigation when set as such, and wherever you use the social icons widget.

  • Name - the profile's or network's name
  • URL - the URL the social icon will lead to. Must be a valid URL format like http://domain.ext for the icon to display, with the following exceptions:
    • rss - when URL is set to "rss" your RSS feed URL will be used
  • Icon - the icon that will appear on the link or button
  • Color - the social icon's color
  • Title - the title will appear in a tooltip when hovering the icon
